Over 100 Million Nigerians Lack Secure and Verifiable Identity

In Nigeria, over 100 million people remain without a trusted digital identity, preventing access to healthcare, banking, social welfare, and economic opportunities. This identity gap hampers national security, weakens financial inclusion, and limits effective governance, disproportionately affecting rural communities, low-income earners, and marginalized groups.

This is Where AIG Comes In

AIG was designed to bridge Nigeria’s identity gap by providing a scalable, secure, and verifiable digital identity system tailored to national needs. Built on global best practices and robust security standards, AIG enables governments to deliver trusted digital identities, ensuring financial inclusion, efficient public service delivery, and enhanced national security. AIG remains committed to empowering individuals and strengthening economies by leveraging cutting-edge technology for secure and inclusive identity management.
