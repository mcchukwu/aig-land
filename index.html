Mission Statement

AIG is designed to give Nigeria full ownership and control over a trusted, verifiable digital identity system that meets the nation’s unique needs. Our scalable and interoperable technology ensures transparency, adaptability, and compliance, allowing seamless integration with government services, financial institutions, and social programs while maintaining strict data security and privacy. With AIG, every Nigerian regardless of location or background can access essential services, participate in the digital economy, and be recognized in a secure, inclusive, and future-ready identity ecosystem.
